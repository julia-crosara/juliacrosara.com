Breaking Free Series

In an effort to reinvigorate my painting practice and get unstuck, I started working with paint in novel ways and gave chance a leading role in the process. The technique I enjoyed the most involved randomly pouring paint on repurposed works on paper; indiscriminately pressing them together from different angles; and then hastily pulling them apart.

The images that surfaced were startling at times and left me wondering if I was dialoguing with my subconscious mind. Speculation aside, I finished up the series with renewed confidence that I could, in fact, break free of inopportune creative blocks and self-imposed limitations.

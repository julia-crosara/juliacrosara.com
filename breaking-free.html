About

Julia is an abstract painter from Chicago who currently lives in the Seattle area. She has a BFA from the University of Illinois at Chicago's School of Art & Design.

From 1993-1995, she had the honor of working with Kerry James Marshall, who had a profound impact on her artistic development.

She has been selling her paintings since 1996 and has shown her work in Chicago, Los Angeles, and Seattle.

Julia is a curious, intuitive artist who likes to experiment with different painting styles and techniques. She draws inspiration from poetry, music, dreams, and the beauty of the Pacific Northwest.

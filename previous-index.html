About Julia

My approach to making art is playful and spontaneous. I start each painting session with an open mind and respond to the materials in the moment. Each painting is comprised of many layers, which adds depth and complexity to the work. The process is dynamic, experiential, and full of surprises.

I earned a BFA in Painting from the School of Art & Design at the University of Illinois at Chicago and BA in Art History from New York University. I have been practicing for over 20 years and have shown my work in Chicago, Los Angeles, and the Pacific Northwest.
